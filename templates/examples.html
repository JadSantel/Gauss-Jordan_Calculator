Gauss–Jordan Elimination

Worked Examples

Two fully solved linear systems with every elementary row operation shown.

Example 1

Solve the 3 × 3 system

Given system:

\[ \begin{cases} 2x_1 + x_2 - x_3 = 8 \\ -3x_1 - x_2 + 2x_3 = -11 \\ -2x_1 + x_2 + 2x_3 = -3 \end{cases} \]

Step 0 · Form the augmented matrix

\[ [A|\mathbf{b}] = \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 2 & 1 & -1 & 8 \\ -3 & -1 & 2 & -11 \\ -2 & 1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 1 · Partial Pivoting (column 1)

Largest absolute value in column 1 is \(|-3|=3\) in row 2. Swap \(R_1 \leftrightarrow R_2\):

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -3 & -1 & 2 & -11 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 & 8 \\ -2 & 1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 2 · Normalize \(R_1\) (divide by \(-3\))

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 \div (-3)\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & -\tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{11}{3} \\ 2 & 1 & -1 & 8 \\ -2 & 1 & 2 & -3 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 3 · Eliminate column 1 from \(R_2\) and \(R_3\)

\(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2\,R_1\)

\[ R_2: \quad \begin{pmatrix}2 - 2(1)\\1 - 2(\tfrac{1}{3})\\-1-2(-\tfrac{2}{3})\\8-2(\tfrac{11}{3})\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}0\\\tfrac{1}{3}\\\tfrac{1}{3}\\\tfrac{2}{3}\end{pmatrix} \]

\(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - (-2)\,R_1 = R_3 + 2\,R_1\)

\[ R_3: \quad \begin{pmatrix}-2+2(1)\\1+2(\tfrac{1}{3})\\2+2(-\tfrac{2}{3})\\-3+2(\tfrac{11}{3})\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}0\\\tfrac{5}{3}\\\tfrac{2}{3}\\\tfrac{13}{3}\end{pmatrix} \] \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & -\tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{11}{3} \\ 0 & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} \\ 0 & \tfrac{5}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{13}{3} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 4 · Normalize \(R_2\) (divide by \(\tfrac{1}{3}\), i.e., multiply by 3)

\(R_2 \leftarrow 3\,R_2\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & -\tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{11}{3} \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & \tfrac{5}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{13}{3} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 5 · Eliminate column 2 from \(R_1\) and \(R_3\)

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - \tfrac{1}{3}\,R_2\)

\[ R_1: \quad \left(1,\;0,\;-\tfrac{2}{3}-\tfrac{1}{3},\;\tfrac{11}{3}-\tfrac{2}{3}\right) = \left(1,\;0,\;-1,\;3\right) \]

\(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - \tfrac{5}{3}\,R_2\)

\[ R_3: \quad \left(0,\;0,\;\tfrac{2}{3}-\tfrac{5}{3},\;\tfrac{13}{3}-\tfrac{10}{3}\right) = \left(0,\;0,\;-1,\;1\right) \] \[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 & 1 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 6 · Normalize \(R_3\) (divide by \(-1\))

\(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 \div (-1)\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 7 · Eliminate column 3 from \(R_1\) and \(R_2\)

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - (-1)\,R_3 = R_1 + R_3\)

\[R_1 = (1,0,0,\;3+(-1)) = (1,0,0,2)\]

\(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - (1)\,R_3\)

\[R_2 = (0,1,0,\;2-(-1)) = (0,1,0,3)\]

Final RREF

\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \]

Solution:

\[ x_1 = 2, \quad x_2 = 3, \quad x_3 = -1 \]

Verify: \(2(2)+(3)-(-1)=8\checkmark\), \(-3(2)-(3)+2(-1)=-11\checkmark\), \(-2(2)+(3)+2(-1)=-3\checkmark\)

Example 2

Solve the 4 × 4 system

Given system:

\[ \begin{cases} x_1 + x_2 + x_3 + x_4 = 10 \\ 2x_1 - x_2 + x_3 + 3x_4 = 13 \\ x_1 + 2x_2 - x_3 + x_4 = 5 \\ 3x_1 + x_2 + 2x_3 - x_4 = 10 \end{cases} \]

Step 0 · Augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \\ 2 & -1 & 1 & 3 & 13 \\ 1 & 2 & -1 & 1 & 5 \\ 3 & 1 & 2 & -1 & 10 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 1 · Partial Pivoting — column 1: pivot in row 4 (\(|3|\) largest)

Swap \(R_1 \leftrightarrow R_4\):

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 3 & 1 & 2 & -1 & 10 \\ 2 & -1 & 1 & 3 & 13 \\ 1 & 2 & -1 & 1 & 5 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 2 · Normalize \(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 / 3\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} & -\tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{10}{3} \\ 2 & -1 & 1 & 3 & 13 \\ 1 & 2 & -1 & 1 & 5 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 10 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 3 · Eliminate column 1 from \(R_2, R_3, R_4\)

\(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_1,\quad R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_1,\quad R_4 \leftarrow R_4 - R_1\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} & -\tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{10}{3} \\ 0 & -\tfrac{5}{3} & -\tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{11}{3} & \tfrac{19}{3} \\ 0 & \tfrac{5}{3} & -\tfrac{5}{3} & \tfrac{4}{3} & \tfrac{5}{3} \\ 0 & \tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{4}{3} & \tfrac{20}{3} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 4 · Normalize \(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 \div (-\tfrac{5}{3})\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{2}{3} & -\tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{10}{3} \\ 0 & 1 & \tfrac{1}{5} & -\tfrac{11}{5} & -\tfrac{19}{5} \\ 0 & \tfrac{5}{3} & -\tfrac{5}{3} & \tfrac{4}{3} & \tfrac{5}{3} \\ 0 & \tfrac{2}{3} & \tfrac{1}{3} & \tfrac{4}{3} & \tfrac{20}{3} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 5 · Eliminate column 2 from \(R_1, R_3, R_4\)

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - \tfrac{1}{3}R_2\), \(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - \tfrac{5}{3}R_2\), \(R_4 \leftarrow R_4 - \tfrac{2}{3}R_2\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & \tfrac{3}{5} & \tfrac{2}{5} & \tfrac{23}{5} \\ 0 & 1 & \tfrac{1}{5} & -\tfrac{11}{5} & -\tfrac{19}{5} \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & 5 & 8 \\ 0 & 0 & \tfrac{1}{5} & \tfrac{14}{5} & \tfrac{46}{5} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 6 · Normalize \(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 \div (-2)\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & \tfrac{3}{5} & \tfrac{2}{5} & \tfrac{23}{5} \\ 0 & 1 & \tfrac{1}{5} & -\tfrac{11}{5} & -\tfrac{19}{5} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -\tfrac{5}{2} & -4 \\ 0 & 0 & \tfrac{1}{5} & \tfrac{14}{5} & \tfrac{46}{5} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 7 · Eliminate column 3 from \(R_1, R_2, R_4\)

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - \tfrac{3}{5}R_3\), \(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - \tfrac{1}{5}R_3\), \(R_4 \leftarrow R_4 - \tfrac{1}{5}R_3\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & \tfrac{19}{10} & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -\tfrac{17}{10} & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -\tfrac{5}{2} & -4 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \tfrac{33}{10} & 10 \end{array}\right] \]

Step 8 · Normalize \(R_4 \leftarrow R_4 \div \tfrac{33}{10}\)

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & \tfrac{19}{10} & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -\tfrac{17}{10} & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -\tfrac{5}{2} & -4 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \tfrac{100}{33} \end{array}\right] \]

Step 9 · Eliminate column 4 from \(R_1, R_2, R_3\)

\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - \tfrac{19}{10}R_4\), \(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + \tfrac{17}{10}R_4\), \(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + \tfrac{5}{2}R_4\)

Final RREF

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \tfrac{41}{33} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & \tfrac{70}{33} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & \tfrac{119}{33} \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \tfrac{100}{33} \end{array}\right] \]

Solution:

\[ x_1 = \tfrac{41}{33},\quad x_2 = \tfrac{70}{33},\quad x_3 = \tfrac{119}{33},\quad x_4 = \tfrac{100}{33} \]
